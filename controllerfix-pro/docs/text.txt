### Service descriptions

**Stick drift repair:** Standard repair service using proven techniques to restore normal stick function without replacement. Recommended as the default stick drift solution.

**OEM joystick replacement:** Replaces the thumbstick module with a standard-style replacement designed to restore normal controller performance. Available for PS5 DualSense, PS5 DualSense Edge, Xbox Series X|S, PS4, Xbox One, and Xbox Elite Series controllers.

**TMR joystick replacement:** Uses advanced drift-resistant joystick technology with electromagnet Hall effect sensors. Positioned as a premium option for customers who want improved durability and precision. More resistant to stick drift. Available for PS5 DualSense, PS5 DualSense Edge, Xbox Series X|S, PS4, Xbox One, and Xbox Elite Series controllers.

**Cleaning service:** Basic cleaning add-on that can be paired with any repair service ($10). Also available as a standalone deep cleaning service ($10) — same pricing, bundled for upsell value.

### Pricing rules

- Transparent fixed pricing will be shown publicly on all service pages
- Multiple issues on the same controller use add-on pricing per issue
- Customers can choose between standard stick drift repair, OEM joystick replacement, or TMR joystick replacement
- TMR joystick replacement is positioned as the premium, drift-resistant upgrade from standard stick drift repair
- Cleaning service can be added as an optional add-on during booking
- "Starting from" language can be used for premium models (DualSense Edge, Xbox Elite Series) to account for potential model-specific cost variations
